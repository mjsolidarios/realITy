Speakers

MIKEEL ARAÑA

Mikeel Araña is a marketing director and designer at FUEL.ph. His passion is visual communication. He loves words, shapes, lines, colours, textures, images, and how they all work together to communicate a message. He also loves how design is a tool for touching people's lives and in shaping a better future together.

PRIM PAYPON

Prim Paypon is a Filipino dreamagineer who founded The Dream Project PH – a non-profit, volunteer-based organization that helps build the nation by building, shaping and enabling collective Filipino dreams among underserved and underprivileged communities through creative education, social design innovation, youth voluntraining and community collaboration.

KING CALDERA

Kristopher King Caldera is currently a general manager and director at MAYAD Studios. He's passionate for directing well-balanced wedding videos that feature mixed emotions–fun shots interwoven with heartfelt ones in a footage that is both candid and cleverly calculated.
